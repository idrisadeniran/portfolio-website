Intro

What I am all about

I am Idris Adesina Adeniran I hold a bachelor’s degree in Management and Accounting. Although I worked with a lot of numbers and data during my undergraduate days, data analysis wasn’t exactly on my radar. I came across data analysis on one of the online learning platforms and it was like love at first sight.

I started self-learning Excel for data analysis, and cleaning data just for fun. Soon, I took up personal projects then evolved to learning SQL, Tableau and R programming. Today, I’m fortunate to say that I am doing what I love and I am good at it.

I'm not a very decisive person, so making decisions based on gut feelings is not my thing. Data, on the other hand, never lies, and allows you to make informed decisions.

Also having the opportunity to leverage current and historical data to make predictions about future events through predictive analysis is fascinating and makes me feel like Doctor Strange from the Avengers movie (but without the cape) !!!
